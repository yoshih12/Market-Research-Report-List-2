<p><p>Inertial sensing systems market includes Attitude Heading and Reference Systems (AHRS), Inertial Navigation Systems (INSs), and Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs). AHRS provide orientation information for aircraft, spacecraft, and other vehicles. INSs utilize IMUs to calculate position, velocity, and attitude. IMUs measure linear and angular motion using accelerometers and gyroscopes. These systems are used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and consumer electronics for navigation, motion tracking, and stabilization applications.</p></p>
