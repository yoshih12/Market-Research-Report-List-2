<p><strong>The Fully Welded Plate Heat Exchangers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plate-and-Block HE</li><li>Plate-and-Shell HE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fully Welded Plate Heat Exchangers come in two main types: Plate-and-Block HE and Plate-and-Shell HE. Plate-and-Block Heat Exchangers consist of plates stacked together with connections on opposite sides. These are ideal for high-pressure applications due to their robust design. On the other hand, Plate-and-Shell Heat Exchangers feature a welded plate pack encased in a pressure shell. They are best suited for applications requiring compactness and efficiency. Both types offer efficient heat transfer and are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and HVAC.</p></p>
