<p><p>Electric valve positioners are devices used to accurately control the position of a valve in response to a control signal. There are two main types of electric valve positioners - single acting and double acting positioners. </p><p>Single acting positioners use air pressure to control the valve position in one direction, while double acting positioners use air pressure to control the valve position in both directions. Single acting positioners are typically used for simpler applications, while double acting positioners are used for more complex control systems that require greater accuracy and precision.</p></p>
