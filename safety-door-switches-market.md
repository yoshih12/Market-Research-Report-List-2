<p><p>Safety door switches come in two main types: mechanical switches and non-contact switches. Mechanical switches require physical contact to open or close the switch, making them reliable but prone to wear and tear. Non-contact switches use technologies such as magnetic fields or infrared sensors to detect when the door is open or closed without physical contact, reducing the risk of damage and increasing longevity. Both types of switches are important in ensuring the safety of machinery and equipment.</p></p>
