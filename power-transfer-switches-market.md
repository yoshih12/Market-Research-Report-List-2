<p><p>Power transfer switches are devices used to switch power sources between utility power and backup power in case of an outage. There are two main types of power transfer switches: Automatic and Manual. </p><p>Automatic transfer switches are designed to switch power sources automatically without any human intervention, ensuring seamless transition during power outages. On the other hand, manual transfer switches require the user to physically switch the power source, which can be more time-consuming and less convenient. Both types play a crucial role in providing uninterrupted power supply during emergencies.</p></p>
