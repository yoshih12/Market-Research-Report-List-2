<p><p>ArF Dry Resist market can be categorized into two main types: Line Resist and Space Resist. Line Resist is used to define narrow lines on the substrate, making it suitable for applications where precise patterns need to be created. On the other hand, Space Resist is designed to create larger gaps or spaces between lines on the substrate, allowing for more flexibility in pattern design. Both market types cater to different needs and specifications in the semiconductor industry, providing a range of options for manufacturers.</p></p>
