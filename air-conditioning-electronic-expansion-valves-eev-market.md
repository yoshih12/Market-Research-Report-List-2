<p><p>Air Conditioning Electronic Expansion Valves (EEV) are essential components in regulating the flow of refrigerant in air conditioning systems. There are two main types of EEV markets - Electromagnetic EEV and Electric EEV. Electromagnetic EEV control the refrigerant flow using a solenoid coil, while Electric EEV uses a stepper motor for precise regulation. Both types offer efficient and accurate control of refrigerant flow, providing better performance and energy savings in air conditioning systems.</p></p>
