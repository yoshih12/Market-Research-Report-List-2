<p><p>Welding flux is classified into three main types: organic, inorganic, and resin-based. Organic flux contains materials derived from natural sources and is known for its excellent cleaning properties. Inorganic flux, on the other hand, is made from mineral-based substances and provides good protection against oxygen contamination. Resin flux is a combination of organic and inorganic components, offering a balance of cleaning and protective properties. Each type of welding flux caters to different welding applications and requirements in the metal fabrication industry.</p></p>
