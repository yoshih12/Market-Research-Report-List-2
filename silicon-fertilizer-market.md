<p><strong>The Silicon Fertilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-soluble Type</li><li>Citrate-soluble Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicon fertilizer market can be categorized into two major types: water-soluble and citrate-soluble. Water-soluble silicon fertilizers are easily dissolved in water, allowing for quick and efficient absorption by plants. On the other hand, citrate-soluble silicon fertilizers are soluble in citric acid, which helps in improving the availability of silicon to plants. Both types of silicon fertilizers play a crucial role in enhancing the growth, development, and overall health of plants by providing them with essential nutrients.</p></p>
