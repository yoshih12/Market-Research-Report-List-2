<p><p>Butyl rubber is a type of synthetic rubber known for its impermeability to gases, making it ideal for use in products such as tires, pharmaceutical stoppers, and sealants. There are three main types of butyl rubber on the market: regular butyl rubber, chlorinated butyl rubber, and brominated butyl rubber. Regular butyl rubber offers good flexibility and low gas permeability, while chlorinated butyl rubber provides improved compatibility with other materials. Brominated butyl rubber offers enhanced resistance to heat and chemicals compared to regular butyl rubber.</p></p>
