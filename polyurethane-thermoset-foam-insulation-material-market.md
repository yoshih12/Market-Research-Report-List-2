<p><p>Polyurethane thermoset foam insulation material is available in two main types: rigid and flexible. Rigid foam insulation is typically used in construction applications where a greater degree of strength and durability is required, such as in walls or roofs. On the other hand, flexible foam insulation is more commonly used in applications where conformability and ease of installation are important, such as in pipes or ductwork. Both types of insulation offer excellent thermal efficiency and can help reduce energy costs in buildings.</p></p>
