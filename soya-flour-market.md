<p><strong>The Soya Flour Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural, or Full-Fat Soya Flour</li><li>Low-Fat Soya Flour</li><li>Defatted Soya Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soya flour is available in three main types: natural or full-fat soya flour, low-fat soya flour, and defatted soya flour. Natural or full-fat soya flour contains the natural fat content of the soybean, which enhances flavor and texture in baked goods. Low-fat soya flour has had some of the fat removed, making it a healthier option with lower fat content. Defatted soya flour has had most of the fat removed, making it highest in protein and fiber content, ideal for use in high-protein diets.</p></p>
