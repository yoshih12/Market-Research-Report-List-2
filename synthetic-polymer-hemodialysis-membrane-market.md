<p><p>Synthetic Polymer Hemodialysis Membrane Market consists of Low Flux Membrane and High Flux Membrane types. On the other hand, High Flux Membrane permits the passage of larger molecules as well, providing better clearance of toxins and improving treatment outcomes for patients with more complex or severe conditions. Both types play a crucial role in hemodialysis by facilitating the removal of waste and excess fluids from the blood.</p></p>
