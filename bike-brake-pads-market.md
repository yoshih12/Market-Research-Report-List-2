<p><p>Bike brake pads come in two main market types - Non-Threaded Post and Threaded Post. Non-threaded post brake pads are designed to be attached to the brake caliper without the need for a threaded post. They are easier to install and adjust. On the other hand, Threaded Post brake pads require a threaded post on the brake caliper for installation. While they may be a bit more secure, they can be more challenging to set up and adjust properly.</p></p>
