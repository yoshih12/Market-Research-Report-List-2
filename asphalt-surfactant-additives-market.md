<p><p>Asphalt surfactant additives are chemicals added to asphalt mixes to improve their performance and properties. Cationic additives have a positively charged surface which helps in better adhesion and bonding between asphalt and aggregates. On the other hand, anionic additives have a negatively charged surface which aids in reducing the viscosity of asphalt mixes and improving workability. Both types of additives play a crucial role in enhancing the overall durability, stability, and performance of asphalt pavements.</p></p>
