<p><p>Point-of-care molecular diagnostics market types include PCR-based, Genetic Sequencing-based, Hybridization-based, and Microarray-based tests. PCR-based tests amplify DNA samples to detect the presence of specific genetic material. Genetic Sequencing-based tests analyze an individual's genetic code to identify mutations or disease risks. Hybridization-based tests use probes to bind to specific DNA sequences for detection. Microarray-based tests analyze multiple genes simultaneously for various diseases or conditions. These technologies provide rapid and accurate results at the point of care, guiding timely treatment decisions.</p></p>
