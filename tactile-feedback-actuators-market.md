<p><p>Tactile Feedback Actuators are devices that provide physical feedback to users in the form of vibrations or movements. Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators use an unbalanced motor to generate vibrations, while Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs) use a mass attached to a spring to create vibrations. Other types of actuators in the market include piezoelectric actuators and solenoid actuators. Each type of actuator has its own unique characteristics and applications in industries such as consumer electronics and automotive.</p></p>
