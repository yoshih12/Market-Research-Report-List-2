<p><p>The suspension system market includes various types such as Air Suspension System, Hydraulic Suspension System, Electromagnetic Suspension System, and Electro-hydraulic Suspension System. Air suspension systems utilize compressed air to adjust the height and stiffness of the vehicle. Hydraulic suspension systems use hydraulic fluid to provide a smoother ride and better handling. Electromagnetic suspension systems use magnets to adjust the suspension in real-time for optimal performance. Electro-hydraulic suspension systems combine hydraulic and electronic components for improved comfort and stability while driving.</p></p>
