<p><p>In the weak power relay market, there are two main types of relays: Closed Type and Open Type. Closed Type relays are sealed units that offer protection against dust, moisture, and other contaminants. They are ideal for harsh environments where reliability is crucial. On the other hand, Open Type relays are exposed units that are more cost-effective and easier to maintain. They are commonly used in indoor applications where protection from external elements is not a significant concern.</p></p>
