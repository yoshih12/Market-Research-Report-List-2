<p><p>Extra virgin olive oil is classified into different market types based on their production methods and quality. Cold pressed olive oil is extracted without heat or chemicals, resulting in a more pure and higher quality product. Flavored olive oil is infused with herbs, spices, or other ingredients for added taste. Virgin olive oil is slightly lower in quality than extra virgin, with a higher level of acidity. Blended olive oil is a mix of different types of olive oils, often used for cooking or lower quality products.</p></p>
