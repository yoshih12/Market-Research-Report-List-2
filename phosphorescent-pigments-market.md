<p><strong>The Phosphorescent Pigments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Persistence Phosphorescent Pigment</li><li>Long Persistence Phosphorescent Pigment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Short persistence phosphorescent pigments have a quick decay time, emitting light for a brief period after being exposed to light. These pigments are ideal for applications where a short burst of light is needed, such as emergency signs or safety markings. On the other hand, long persistence phosphorescent pigments have a slower decay time and can emit light for an extended period after being charged. They are commonly used in applications where a continuous glow is required, such as in watches, toys, or home decor items.</p></p>
