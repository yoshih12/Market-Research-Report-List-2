TACSR (Thermal Resistant Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced) is designed for high temperature operation. TACSR/AW (Aluminum Clad Steel Reinforced) has improved corrosion resistance. STACIR/AW (Super Thermal Alloy Coated Aluminum Conductor Alloy Reinforced) offers high current-carrying capacity. TACSR/TW (Transposed Thermal Resistant Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced) is used for transmission lines. Other types may include variations in materials and configurations to suit specific requirements in the heat resistant alloy conductor market.</p></p>
