<p><ul><li>Transmitter IC</li><li>Receiver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless Charging Integrated Circuits (ICs) market is segmented into two main types: Transmitter IC and Receiver IC. Transmitter ICs are responsible for converting electrical power into an electromagnetic field, which is then received and converted back into electrical power by the Receiver IC. Transmitter ICs are used in charging pads or stations, while Receiver ICs are integrated into devices such as smartphones or wearables. Both types of ICs play a crucial role in enabling wireless charging technology to function efficiently and conveniently.</p></p>
