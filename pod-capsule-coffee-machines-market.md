<p><p>Pod & Capsule Coffee Machines come in two main market types - Closed Source System and Open Source System. Closed Source System machines require proprietary pods or capsules made by specific brands, limiting consumer choice but also ensuring quality and consistency. On the other hand, Open Source System machines allow for the use of a variety of pods or capsules from different brands, offering more flexibility but potentially sacrificing on the quality and consistency of the final coffee product.</p></p>
