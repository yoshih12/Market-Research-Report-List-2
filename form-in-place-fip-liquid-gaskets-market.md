<p><p>Form-in-Place (FIP) Liquid Gaskets are offered in two main types: Conductive Form-In-Place Gaskets and Non-Conductive Form-In-Place Gaskets. Conductive FIP gaskets are ideal for applications requiring electrical conductivity, such as in electronic devices. On the other hand, Non-Conductive FIP gaskets are used in applications where electrical insulation is required, such as in automotive components. Both types offer the advantages of easy application, reduced waste, and improved sealing performance compared to traditional pre-cut gaskets.</p></p>
