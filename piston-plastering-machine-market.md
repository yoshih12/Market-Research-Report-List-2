<p><ul><li>Single Piston</li><li>Double Piston</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piston plastering machines are categorized into single piston and double piston types based on the number of pistons they have. Single piston machines have one piston that pushes the plaster material through the nozzle, while double piston machines have two pistons working in tandem to expedite the plastering process. Single piston machines are typically smaller and more suitable for smaller-scale projects, while double piston machines are more powerful and efficient, making them ideal for larger projects that require faster plaster application.</p></p>
