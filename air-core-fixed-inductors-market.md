<p><p>Air Core Fixed Inductors are classified into two main types - Multi-layer Type and Wire Wound Type. Multi-layer Type inductors are constructed using multiple layers of coiled wire, providing higher inductance values. Wire Wound Type inductors are constructed by winding a single wire around a core, offering lower inductance values. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications based on their specific characteristics, such as frequency response, resistance, and power handling capabilities.</p></p>
