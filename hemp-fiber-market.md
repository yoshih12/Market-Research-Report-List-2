<p><ul><li>Long (bast) Fibers</li><li>Short (core) Fibers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hemp fiber market is divided into two main types: long (bast) fibers and short (core) fibers. Long fibers come from the outer stalk of the hemp plant and are known for their strength and durability. They are commonly used in textiles, ropes, and paper products. On the other hand, short fibers come from the inner part of the stalk and are used for products like insulation, animal bedding, and biofuels. Both types of fibers serve different purposes in various industries within the hemp market.</p></p>
