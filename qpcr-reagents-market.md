<p><ul><li>Dye-based Reagents</li><li>Probe & Primer-based Reagents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>qPCR reagents are essential components used in quantitative polymerase chain reaction (qPCR) techniques for DNA amplification and quantification. The market for qPCR reagents is segmented into dye-based reagents, probe-based reagents, and primer-based reagents. Dye-based reagents utilize fluorescent dyes that bind to double-stranded DNA for quantification, while probe-based reagents use specific probes that bind to target sequences for detection. Primer-based reagents rely on specific primers that anneal to complementary DNA sequences for amplification. Each type of reagent offers unique advantages and is used in various applications within the qPCR field.</p></p>
