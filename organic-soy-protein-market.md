<p><ul><li>Soy Protein Concentrates</li><li>Soy Protein Isolates</li><li>Soy Protein Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic Soy Protein market consists of three main types: soy protein concentrates, soy protein isolates, and soy protein flour. Soy protein concentrates are made by removing most of the fats and carbohydrates, leaving a high protein content. Soy protein isolates undergo further processing to remove even more fats and carbohydrates, resulting in a product with a very high protein content. Soy protein flour is made by finely grinding soybeans into a powder. Each type of soy protein offers different levels of protein concentration and functionality for various food products.</p></p>
