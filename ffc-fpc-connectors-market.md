<p><p>FFC (Flexible Flat Cable) connectors are thin, flat cables with parallel copper conductors that are laminated between two layers of insulation. They are commonly used to connect electronic components in tight spaces due to their flexibility and space-saving design. FPC (Flexible Printed Circuit) connectors are similar to FFC connectors but are typically used in applications where the cable needs to bend or twist. Both FFC and FPC connectors are widely used in various industries such as automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
