<p><p>Bottle nipples are categorized based on their flow rates, which determine how quickly milk or formula is dispensed to the baby. Slow flow nipples are designed for newborns or breastfed babies to prevent overfeeding. Medium flow nipples are suitable for older babies who can handle a slightly faster flow. Fast flow nipples are for older babies or those who have trouble sucking. Variable flow nipples allow parents to adjust the flow rate to suit their baby's needs. These different types cater to various age groups and feeding preferences.</p></p>
