Conductive plastic potentiometers offer a cost-effective solution with good linearity and reliability, making them popular in consumer electronics. The "other" market includes specialty potentiometers with unique characteristics such as wirewound construction or hybrid technology, catering to specific industry needs. Each market type has its own strengths and applications within the multi-turn potentiometer industry.</p></p>
