<p><p>Cold Rolled Sheet Piles are offered in various types which include Spiral Weld Pipe, Electric Resistance Weld, and Double Submerged Arc Weld. Spiral Weld Pipe is created by spirally welding steel plates to form a pipe structure. Electric Resistance Weld involves the use of an electric current to heat and bond the edges of the steel plate. Double Submerged Arc Weld involves welding the steel plate using two submerged arc welding processes to create a strong and durable sheet pile structure.</p></p>
