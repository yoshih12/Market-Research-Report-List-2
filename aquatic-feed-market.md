<p><strong>The Aquatic Feed Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mash Feed</li><li>Pellet Feed</li><li>Expanded Feed</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aquatic feed market types include mash feed, pellet feed, expanded feed, and other specialized feeds. Mash feed is a mixture of ground ingredients that are easily digestible by aquatic species. Pellet feed is formed by compacting mash feed into small pellets for easier handling and feeding. Expanded feed is produced by extruding the feed ingredients under high temperature and pressure to create a more stable and nutritious product. Other specialized feeds may include floating feeds, sinking feeds, or medicated feeds designed for specific aquatic species and their needs.</p></p>
