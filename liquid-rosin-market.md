Softwood Type is derived from pine trees and is known for its high viscosity and excellent adhesive properties. Mixed Type combines the characteristics of both softwood and hardwood types, offering a balance of properties. Hardwood Type, derived from deciduous trees, is valued for its lower acidity and lighter color.</p></p>
