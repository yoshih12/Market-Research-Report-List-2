<p><p>Automatic titrators are widely used in various industries for determining the concentration of a substance in a solution. There are different types of automatic titrators available in the market, including potential titration, coulomb titration, and other specialized titration methods. Potential titration involves measuring the potential difference between two electrodes in a reaction, while coulomb titration measures the amount of electric charge passed through a solution during a titration. Other specialized titration methods include amperometric titration and conductometric titration, each offering unique advantages for different applications.</p></p>
