<p><p>Gas meters are devices used to measure the volume of gas consumed by a household or commercial property. There are two main types of gas meters - wet gas meters and dry gas meters. Wet gas meters use a liquid for sealing and lubrication, while dry gas meters do not require any liquid. Both types of meters have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications. Wet gas meters are more accurate in measuring low flow rates, while dry gas meters are more cost-effective and require less maintenance.</p></p>
