<p><p>The EV cables market can be divided into two main types based on charging methods: AC Charging and DC Charging. On the other hand, DC Charging involves the use of direct current to provide faster charging solutions mainly found at fast charging stations along highways or at commercial locations. Both types of charging options cater to different charging needs of electric vehicle users.</p></p>
