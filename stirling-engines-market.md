<p><strong>The Stirling Engines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alpha Stirling Engines</li><li>Beta Stirling Engines</li><li>Gamma Stirling Engines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stirling engines are categorized into three main types based on their design and operating principles: Alpha, Beta, and Gamma. Alpha Stirling engines have two separate cylinders for the hot and cold gases, while Beta Stirling engines use a displacer to move the gas between the two ends of a single cylinder. Gamma Stirling engines have a single cylinder with a displacer that is not sealed, allowing gas to flow freely between the hot and cold ends. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
