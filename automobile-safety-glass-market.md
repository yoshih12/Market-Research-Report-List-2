<p><p>Automobile safety glass market consists of various types such as tempered glass, laminated glass, and others. Tempered glass is a type of safety glass that is processed to increase its strength, making it less likely to break into sharp and dangerous pieces upon impact. Laminated glass is made by sandwiching a layer of polyvinyl butyral between two layers of glass, which holds the glass together upon impact. Other types of safety glass in the market include heated glass and acoustic glass, offering additional safety features for automobile passengers.</p></p>
