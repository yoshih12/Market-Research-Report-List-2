<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Discrete Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Pole-Single-Throw (SPST)</li><li>Single-Pole-Double-Throw (SPDT)</li><li>Single-Pole-n-Throw (SPnT)</li><li>n-Pole-n-Throw (nPnT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The discrete switch market is categorized into four main types based on the number of poles and throws in the switch mechanism. Single-Pole-Single-Throw (SPST) switches have one input and one output. Single-Pole-Double-Throw (SPDT) switches have one input and two outputs. Single-Pole-n-Throw (SPnT) switches have one input and multiple outputs. Lastly, n-Pole-n-Throw (nPnT) switches have multiple inputs and outputs, offering more complex switching configurations for various applications.</p></p>
