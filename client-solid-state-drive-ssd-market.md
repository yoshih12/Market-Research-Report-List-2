<p><p>Client Solid-State Drive (SSD) market includes various types based on NAND technology – Multi-Level Cell (MLC) NAND, Triple-Level Cell (TLC) NAND, and 3D NAND. MLC NAND offers higher endurance and performance but is more expensive. TLC NAND is cost-effective with lower endurance compared to MLC. 3D NAND technology stacks memory cells vertically, offering higher capacity and faster speeds. Each type caters to different needs and budgets in the client SSD market, providing consumers with a range of options to choose from.</p></p>
