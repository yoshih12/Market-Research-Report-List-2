<p><p>Sperm separation systems are used for various applications such as in vitro fertilization and sperm analysis. There are three main types of sperm separation systems on the market: On-chip systems, Electrophoresis systems, and Dielectrophoresis systems. On-chip systems involve separating sperm on a microchip, while Electrophoresis systems use an electric field to separate sperm based on charge. Dielectrophoresis systems separate sperm based on their dielectric properties. Each type of system has its own advantages and applications in the field of reproductive technology.</p></p>
