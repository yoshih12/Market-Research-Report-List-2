<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Lubricanting Oil Additive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Viscosity Index Improvers</li><li>Dispersants</li><li>Detergents</li><li>Inhibitor/Antiwear Agents</li><li>Antioxidants</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lubricating oil additives can be categorized into different types based on their functions and properties. Viscosity Index Improvers improve the viscosity-temperature relationship of the oil. Dispersants prevent the formation of deposits and sludge in the engine. Detergents help in cleaning the engine components by removing dirt and deposits. Inhibitor/Antiwear Agents reduce friction and wear on metal surfaces. Antioxidants prevent oil degradation due to oxidation. Other additives may include pour point depressants, friction modifiers, and corrosion inhibitors to enhance the performance and longevity of the lubricant.</p></p>
