<p><p>Genetic virus vector market includes various types such as retroviral vectors, adenoviral vectors, adeno-associated viral vectors, and other vectors. Retroviral vectors are commonly used for gene therapy due to their ability to integrate the therapeutic gene into the host cell's genome. Adenoviral vectors are known for their high gene delivery efficiency in various cell types. Adeno-associated viral vectors have gained popularity for their ability to induce long-term gene expression without causing significant immune response. Other market vectors include lentiviral vectors, herpesviral vectors, and rhabdoviral vectors, each with unique advantages for specific gene therapy applications.</p></p>
