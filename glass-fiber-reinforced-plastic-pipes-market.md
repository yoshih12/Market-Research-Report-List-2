<p><p>Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastic (GRP) pipes come in three main types based on the resin used in their composition: Polyester, Vinyl Ester, and Epoxy. Polyester GRP pipes are cost-effective and have good corrosion resistance. Vinyl Ester GRP pipes offer high chemical resistance and thermal properties. Epoxy GRP pipes have exceptional strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals and high temperatures. Each type of GRP pipe caters to specific applications and requirements, providing a versatile and reliable option for various industries.</p></p>
