<p><p>Automotive dry friction materials market consists of three main types: non-asbestos organic friction materials, asbestos friction materials, and low-metallic friction materials. Non-asbestos organic friction materials are made from organic fibers and fillers, offering environmental friendliness and performance. Asbestos friction materials, although now banned in many countries due to health concerns, were commonly used for their heat resistance. Low-metallic friction materials contain a higher percentage of metal fibers, providing better heat dissipation and durability in high-performance applications. Each type offers unique benefits for various automotive applications.</p></p>
