<p><strong>The Servo Drivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse</li><li>Analog</li><li>Network</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Servo drivers are categorized into three main types based on how they receive signals to control the motor: pulse, analog, and network. Pulse servo drivers operate by receiving pulse signals from a motion controller. Analog servo drivers receive continuous voltage or current signals to control the motor. Network servo drivers communicate with the control system through a network connection, such as Ethernet or CAN bus. Each type of servo driver has its own advantages and applications, depending on the specific requirements of the system.</p></p>
