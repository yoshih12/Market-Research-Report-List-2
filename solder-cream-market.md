<p><strong>The Solder Cream Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rosin Based Creams</li><li>Water Soluble Creams</li><li>No-clean Creams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solder cream market is primarily categorized into three types: rosin-based creams, water-soluble creams, and no-clean creams. Rosin-based creams are traditional and provide good electrical conductivity. Water-soluble creams are easy to clean but may leave residues if not properly removed. No-clean creams do not require cleaning after soldering, making them convenient for certain applications. Each type caters to different preferences and requirements in the electronics industry, offering various benefits and considerations to users.</p></p>
