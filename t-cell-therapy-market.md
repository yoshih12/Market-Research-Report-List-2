<p><strong>The T-Cell Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CAR T Cell Therapy</li><li>TCR Therapy</li><li>TIL Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>T-Cell therapy encompasses CAR T cell therapy, TCR therapy, and TIL therapy markets. CAR T cell therapy involves modifying patients' T cells to recognize and attack cancer cells. TCR therapy involves genetically modifying T cells to express a T-cell receptor that can target specific antigens on cancer cells. TIL therapy involves isolating tumor-infiltrating lymphocytes from a patient's tumor, expanding them in a lab, and then infusing them back into the patient to target and destroy cancer cells.</p></p>
