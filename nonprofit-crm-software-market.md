<p><p>Nonprofit CRM software is available in two main market types: cloud-based and premise-based. Cloud-based CRM software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, providing flexibility and scalability for nonprofits. Premise-based CRM software is installed and managed on the organization's own servers. While premise-based software may offer more control over data security, it typically requires higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance.
