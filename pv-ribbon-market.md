<p><strong>The PV Ribbon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Connection</li><li>Bustar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PV Ribbon market can be categorized into two types: connection and busbar. Connection ribbon is used to connect photovoltaic cells in a module, ensuring proper electrical conductivity. Busbar ribbon, on the other hand, is wider and thicker, designed for higher current-carrying capacity. Both types play a crucial role in the efficiency and performance of solar modules. Connection ribbon facilitates the flow of electricity between cells, while busbar ribbon distributes the current across the module for optimal power output.</p></p>
