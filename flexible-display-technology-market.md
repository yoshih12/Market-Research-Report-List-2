<p><p>Flexible Display Technology Market includes three main types: Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED), and Electronic Paper Display (EPD). </p><p>LCD technology uses a liquid crystal solution sandwiched between two layers of glass to display images. OLED technology uses organic compounds that emit light when an electric current passes through them. EPD technology mimics the appearance of traditional ink on paper, making it easy to read in various lighting conditions. Each technology offers unique features and benefits for different applications.</p></p>
