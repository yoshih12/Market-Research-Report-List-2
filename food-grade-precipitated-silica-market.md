<p><p>Food Grade Precipitated Silica Market includes two main types: Highly dispersible silica and Easy dispersible silica. Highly dispersible silica is characterized by having a very fine particle size and high surface area, which allows for better dispersion and suspension in food products. On the other hand, Easy dispersible silica has a larger particle size and lower surface area, making it easier to handle and incorporate into food formulations. Both types of silica are widely used as anti-caking agents, thickeners, and carriers in various food applications.</p></p>
