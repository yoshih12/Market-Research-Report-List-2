Walk-Behind sweepers are more compact and maneuverable, making them suitable for smaller spaces or areas with obstacles. Both types are used for cleaning floors by collecting dust, debris, and other waste, but the choice between the two depends on the size and layout of the space being cleaned.</p></p>
