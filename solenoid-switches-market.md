<p><p>A solenoid switch is a type of electromechanical device that converts electrical energy into mechanical movement. It consists of a coil of wire wrapped around a ferrous core, which when energized, creates a magnetic field that moves a plunger or armature, thereby opening or closing a circuit. Solenoid switches are commonly used in various applications such as automotive, industrial machinery, aerospace, and healthcare equipment.</p><p>The Solenoid Switches Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for automation and advancements in technology driving the adoption of solenoid switches in various industries.
