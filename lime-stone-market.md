<p><p>Limestone market is classified into two main types based on their chemical composition: High-Calcium Limestone and Magnesian Limestone. High-Calcium Limestone contains a high percentage of calcium carbonate, making it suitable for various industrial applications such as construction, agriculture, and water treatment. On the other hand, Magnesian Limestone contains a higher percentage of magnesium carbonate, making it more suitable for use in the production of fertilizers and magnesium products. Both types play a crucial role in different industries due to their unique chemical compositions.</p></p>
