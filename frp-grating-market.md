<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The FRP Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Molded FRP Grating</li><li>Pultruded FRP Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molded FRP grating is created by blending resin with continuous fiberglass strands, resulting in a strong and durable product suitable for various applications such as walkways, platforms, and stair treads. Pultruded FRP grating is manufactured using a process where fiberglass rovings and continuous strands are pulled through a resin bath and then heated and cured, producing a high-strength product with excellent corrosion resistance. Both types of FRP grating are widely used in industries such as construction, marine, and oil & gas due to their lightweight yet robust nature.</p></p>
