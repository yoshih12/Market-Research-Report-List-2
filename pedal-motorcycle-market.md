<p><p>Pedal motorcycle market offers two main types: Two-stroke motorcycles and Four-stroke motorcycles. Two-stroke motorcycles are lighter and simpler, with engines that complete a power cycle in just two strokes of the piston. They are often preferred for their higher power-to-weight ratio and simpler design. On the other hand, four-stroke motorcycles feature more complex engines that complete a power cycle in four strokes. They are known for their smoother operation, better fuel efficiency, and lower emissions. Customers can choose between the two based on their specific preferences and requirements.</p></p>
