<p><p>Optical filters for displays come in three main types - glass, polycarbonate, and acrylic. Glass filters offer high optical clarity and durability, making them ideal for high-end display applications. Polycarbonate filters are lightweight and impact-resistant, suitable for rugged environments. Acrylic filters provide good optical properties and are cost-effective. Each type of filter has its advantages and is chosen based on specific display requirements such as optical performance, durability, weight, and cost.</p></p>
