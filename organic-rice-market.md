<p><ul><li>Polished glutinous rice(sticky rice)</li><li>Indica(long-shaped rice)</li><li>Polished round-grained rice</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic rice market can be divided into three main types based on the variety of rice: polished glutinous rice (sticky rice), Indica (long-shaped rice), and polished round-grained rice. Polished glutinous rice, also known as sticky rice, is popular in Asian cuisine for its sticky texture. Indica rice, with its long shape, is commonly used in dishes such as biryani and sushi. Polished round-grained rice is preferred for its smooth and shiny appearance, making it a popular choice for dishes like risotto and paella.</p></p>
