<p><p>Diamond-like Carbon (DLC) has two main market types: Pure DLC and Mixed DLC. Pure DLC consists of entirely carbon-based coatings, providing high hardness, low friction, and chemical resistance. Mixed DLC, on the other hand, combines DLC with other materials such as metals or ceramics to enhance specific properties like wear resistance or adhesion. Both types offer unique benefits tailored to different industries and applications, making them versatile options for a wide range of products and technologies.</p></p>
