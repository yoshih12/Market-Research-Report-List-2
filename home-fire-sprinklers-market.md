<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Home Fire Sprinklers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet</li><li>Dry</li><li>Preaction</li><li>Deluge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home fire sprinklers can be categorized into four main types: wet, dry, preaction, and deluge systems. Wet sprinkler systems are the most common and reliable, with water constantly in the pipes ready to discharge upon detection of a fire. Dry systems are used in areas prone to freezing temperatures, with water released only when a fire is detected. Preaction systems are typically used in areas with valuable assets, with water released only after a smoke or heat detection system confirms a fire. Deluge systems release a large volume of water over a wide area when a fire is detected, often used in industrial settings.</p></p>
