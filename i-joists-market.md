<p><p>I-joists, also known as engineered wood joists, are used as structural components in the construction industry. They are made by combining engineered wood products like laminated veneer lumber (LVL) or oriented strand board (OSB) with a vertical web to form an I-shaped cross-section. I-joists offer several advantages over traditional solid wood joists, such as increased strength and dimensional stability, as well as being lighter and more cost-effective.</p><p>The I-joists market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period.
