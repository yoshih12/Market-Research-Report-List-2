<p><p>High barrier packaging films market can be classified into various types such as PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), CPP (Cast Polypropylene), BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene), PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol), PLA (Polylactic Acid), and Others. PET films offer excellent barrier properties against moisture and oxygen, while CPP films provide high heat resistance. BOPP films are known for their high clarity and glossiness. PVA films are biodegradable and offer good barrier properties. PLA films are derived from renewable resources and are compostable. Other types may include EVOH (ethylene vinyl alcohol copolymer) and nylon films.</p></p>
