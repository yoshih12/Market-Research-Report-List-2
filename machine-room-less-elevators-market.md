<p><p>Machine Room Less Elevators, also known as MRL elevators, are a type of elevator that eliminates the need for a separate machine room by integrating the motor and other components within the hoistway structure. MRL elevators are becoming increasingly popular in residential and commercial buildings due to their cost-effectiveness and space-saving benefits.</p><p>The Machine Room Less Elevators Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.3% during the forecast period.
