<p><p>Cellulose Acetate Butyrate is categorized into three types based on the content of butyryl groups present in the material. Low Butyryl Content CAB offers enhanced flexibility and adhesion properties, making it suitable for coatings and inks. Medium Butyryl Content CAB provides a balance between flexibility and hardness, ideal for applications such as molded goods and automotive parts. High Butyryl Content CAB offers high durability and impact resistance, making it suitable for products requiring a strong protective layer like eyeglass frames and electronic casings.</p></p>
