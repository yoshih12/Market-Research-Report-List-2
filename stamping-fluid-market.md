<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Oil Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stamping fluid is categorized into water-based and oil-based types within the market. Water-based stamping fluids are environment-friendly, non-toxic, and offer easy cleanup. These fluids are suitable for use on a variety of materials, including aluminum, steel, and copper. On the other hand, oil-based stamping fluids are known for their high lubricity and excellent cooling properties, making them ideal for heavy-duty metal stamping applications. Both types of stamping fluids provide corrosion protection and improved tool life during metal stamping processes.</p></p>
