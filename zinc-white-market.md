<p><p>Zinc White market can be categorized into three main types: Direct Process (America Method), Indirect Process (French Method), and Wet Chemical Process. The Direct Process involves the reaction of zinc metal with acid to produce zinc oxide, while the Indirect Process involves the combustion of zinc vapor to form zinc oxide. The Wet Chemical Process uses a chemical precipitation method to produce zinc oxide. Each method has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of cost, efficiency, and quality of the final product.</p></p>
