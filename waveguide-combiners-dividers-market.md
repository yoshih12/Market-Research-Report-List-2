<p><p>Waveguide combiners/dividers are used in RF and microwave systems to combine or split signals. The 3dB waveguide combiner/divider equally splits input signals into two output ports with a 3dB loss. The 3dB hybrid waveguide combiner/divider combines signals while maintaining a balanced output. Variable power dividers allow for adjustable power ratios between output ports. These types of waveguide combiners/dividers cater to different needs in communication systems, providing options for signal management and distribution.</p></p>
