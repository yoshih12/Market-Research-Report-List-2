<p><p>Annatto market types include Oil-Soluble Annatto, Water-Soluble Annatto, and Emulsified Annatto. Oil-Soluble Annatto is derived from seeds and offers a concentrated color solution suitable for various food products. Water-Soluble Annatto is a water-based coloring agent made from the seed's extracts, often used in beverages and dairy products. Emulsified Annatto is a blend of oil-soluble and water-soluble annatto, providing flexibility in application across a wide range of food and beverage products. Each type offers distinct advantages based on the desired end product.</p></p>
