<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Railway Buffer Stops Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frictional Buffer Stops</li><li>Fixed Buffer Stops</li><li>Hydraulic/Mechanical Buffer Stops</li><li>Wheel Stops</li><li>Folding Buffer Stops</li><li>Special Designed Buffer Stops</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Railway buffer stops come in various types based on their design and functionality. Frictional buffer stops use friction to stop trains. Fixed buffer stops are permanently fixed at the end of tracks. Hydraulic/mechanical buffer stops use hydraulic or mechanical systems to absorb impact. Wheel stops prevent trains from rolling off tracks. Folding buffer stops can be folded down when not in use. Special designed buffer stops are customized for specific rail network requirements. Each type serves a specific purpose in ensuring safety and efficiency in railway operations.</p></p>
