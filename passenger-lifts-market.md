<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Passenger Lifts</li><li>Electric Passenger Lifts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger lifts are categorized into two main types: Hydraulic Passenger Lifts and Electric Passenger Lifts. Hydraulic Passenger Lifts operate using hydraulic power to move between floors, typically in buildings with a low to medium rise. On the other hand, Electric Passenger Lifts are powered by electricity and are commonly used in high-rise buildings. Both types of lifts provide efficient vertical transportation for passengers within buildings, but their mechanisms and applications differ based on the building's height and requirements.</p></p>
