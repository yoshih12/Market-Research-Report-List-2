<p><p>Multi-chamber sputtering systems come in various types including load-lock and batch type markets. Load-lock systems provide a separate chamber for loading and unloading substrates without disrupting the main process chamber, increasing efficiency and reducing downtime. On the other hand, batch type systems allow for multiple substrates to be processed simultaneously in separate chambers, enhancing productivity. Both types cater to different requirements and offer unique advantages in the field of sputtering technology.</p></p>
