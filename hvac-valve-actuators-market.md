<p><p>HVAC valve actuators are classified into various types based on their functionality. Floating actuators control the on/off function of valves, while modulating actuators adjust the valve position continuously. Pneumatic actuators use air pressure to operate valves, while proportional actuators provide precise control over valve positions. Other types of actuators include electric, hydraulic, and digital actuators, each offering unique features to suit different HVAC systems. These different types cater to the diverse needs and requirements of HVAC systems in various industries.</p></p>
