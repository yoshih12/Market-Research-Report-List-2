<p><p>Field-mount temperature transmitters are devices used to convert temperature readings into electrical signals for monitoring and control purposes. Different types of temperature transmitters include Thermistors, which use the change in resistance with temperature, Bimetallic temperature transmitter with two different metal strips that expand at different rates, Resistive Temperature Detectors which work on the principle of resistance change with temperature, Fiber Optic Temperature Transmitters which use fiber optics for accurate temperature readings, and Thermocouples that generate a voltage signal proportional to temperature difference.</p></p>
