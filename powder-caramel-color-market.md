<p><p>Powder caramel color is commonly available in three main market types: Plain Caramel Color, Ammonia Caramel Color, and Ammonia Sulfite Caramel Color. Plain Caramel Color is the most basic form, created by heating sugar. Ammonia Caramel Color is produced by adding ammonia during the processing stage, resulting in a slightly different flavor profile. Ammonia Sulfite Caramel Color is made by incorporating both ammonia and sulfite compounds during production, offering a unique taste and color characteristics. These market types cater to different preferences and applications in the food industry.</p></p>
