<p><strong>The Polyurethane Elastomer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoset PU Elastomers</li><li>Thermoplastic PU Elastomers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane Elastomer Market Types can be classified into Thermoset PU Elastomers and Thermoplastic PU Elastomers. Thermoset PU Elastomers are crosslinked polymers that undergo irreversible chemical reactions during processing, resulting in a stable, durable material. They are commonly used for applications requiring high strength, flexibility, and resistance to heat and chemicals. On the other hand, Thermoplastic PU Elastomers can be melted and reformed multiple times, offering versatility and ease of processing for applications such as injection molding and extrusion.</p></p>
