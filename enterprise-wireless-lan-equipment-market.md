<p><p>In the Enterprise Wireless LAN Equipment market, there are two main types of products: Access Points and WLAN Controllers. Access Points are devices that allow wireless devices to connect to a wired network. They provide the network coverage and capacity needed for multiple devices to connect simultaneously. WLAN Controllers, on the other hand, are centralized devices that manage and control multiple Access Points in a network. They help optimize network performance, ensure security, and provide seamless roaming capabilities for users. Together, these products play a crucial role in providing reliable and efficient wireless connectivity in enterprises.</p></p>
