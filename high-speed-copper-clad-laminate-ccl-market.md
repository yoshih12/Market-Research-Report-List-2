<p><p>High Speed Copper Clad Laminate (CCL) market consists of three main types: Organic Resin Copper Clad Laminate, Metal Substrate, and Ceramic Substrate. Organic Resin CCLs are made of fiberglass and epoxy resin, providing good electrical insulation and mechanical strength. Metal Substrate CCLs have a layer of metal like aluminum for improved thermal conductivity and heat dissipation. Ceramic Substrate CCLs are made of ceramic materials like alumina or aluminum nitride for high thermal conductivity and reliability. Each type caters to specific applications and requirements in high-speed electronic devices.</p></p>
