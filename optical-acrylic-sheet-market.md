<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical acrylic sheets are commonly available in two types: Extruded Acrylic Sheet and Cast Acrylic Sheet. Extruded acrylic sheets are manufactured using a continuous manufacturing process, resulting in a product with uniform thickness and better impact resistance. On the other hand, cast acrylic sheets are made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, resulting in high optical clarity and scratch resistance. Both types of acrylic sheets are widely used in various industries for applications such as signage, displays, and protective barriers.</p></p>
