<p><p>Artificial marble refers to a type of composite material used for surfaces like countertops, floors, and walls, that mimics the appearance of natural marble. The market for artificial marble is mainly divided into two types: solid surface and engineered quartz stone. Solid surface artificial marble is made from a mixture of acrylic resins, minerals, and pigments, offering a durable and versatile option. Engineered quartz stone is made from quartz crystals combined with resin, producing a strong and non-porous surface ideal for various applications.</p></p>
