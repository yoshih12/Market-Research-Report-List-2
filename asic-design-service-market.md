<p><p>Standard Cell Based ASIC market involves the design of custom integrated circuits using pre-designed standard cells for digital logic implementation. This type of ASIC design service offers faster time-to-market and lower cost compared to full-custom design. On the other hand, Gate Array Based ASIC market involves customizing pre-fabricated gate arrays by interconnecting them to create the desired functionality. This type offers more flexibility and customization options but may require longer development time and higher cost. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the ASIC design industry.</p></p>
