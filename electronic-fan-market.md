<p><p>The electronic fan market can be categorized into three main types: Axial Electronic Fans, Centrifugal Electronic Fans, and Other electronic fans. Axial fans are designed with blades that rotate around an axis and are suitable for applications requiring high airflow at low pressures. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, create airflow by accelerating air radially outward and are ideal for applications with high-pressure requirements. The "Other" category includes specialty electronic fans such as brushless DC fans, cross-flow fans, and mixed flow fans, each offering unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
