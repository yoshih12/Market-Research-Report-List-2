<p><p>High Intensity Discharge (HID) Lamps are classified into different types based on the gas used, such as Xenon Gas Discharge Lamp, High-Pressure Gas Discharge Lamp, and Electrodeless Discharge Lamp. Xenon Gas Discharge Lamp provides a bright white light suitable for automotive headlights and stadium lighting. High-Pressure Gas Discharge Lamp produces a high intensity light used in street lighting and stadium lighting. Electrodeless Discharge Lamp uses electromagnetic induction to excite the gas, resulting in energy-efficient lighting for commercial and industrial applications.</p></p>
