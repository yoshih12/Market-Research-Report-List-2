<p><p>Reactive specialty adhesives market consists of four main types: epoxy, modified acrylic, polyurethane, and radiation curable adhesives. Epoxy adhesives are known for their high strength and chemical resistance. Modified acrylic adhesives offer improved flexibility and heat resistance. Polyurethane adhesives are known for their durability and ability to bond to a variety of substrates. Radiation curable adhesives are fast-curing and provide excellent adhesion to plastics and metals. Each type of adhesive has its own unique properties and applications, making them suitable for a wide range of industries and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
