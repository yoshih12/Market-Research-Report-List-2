<p><p>The antidegradant market can be categorized into different types based on their functionalities. Staining antioxidants are used to prevent discoloration and staining in rubber products. Other markets in the antidegradant industry may include products such as UV stabilizers, thermal stabilizers, and metal deactivators, which offer additional protective properties to rubber products against various environmental factors.
