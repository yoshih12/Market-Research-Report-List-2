<p><p>The Lipoprotein A Test Kit market offers two main types of test kits: Immunochromatography and Turbidimetric Inhibition Immuno Assay. Immunochromatography tests use antibodies to detect specific proteins on lipoproteins in a quick and easy-to-read format. Turbidimetric Inhibition Immuno Assay tests measure the presence of a specific lipoprotein by inhibiting its reaction with an antibody and measuring the resulting change in turbidity. Both types of test kits provide accurate and efficient ways to analyze lipoprotein levels in the body.</p></p>
