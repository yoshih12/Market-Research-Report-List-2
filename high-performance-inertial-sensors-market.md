<p><p>High Performance Inertial Sensors Market consists of two main types: Angular Rate Gyro and Linear Accelerometer. Angular Rate Gyro sensors measure the rate of rotation around an axis, providing crucial data for applications requiring precise orientation and positioning. Linear Accelerometer sensors, on the other hand, measure acceleration along a straight line, enabling accurate tracking of movement and acceleration in various devices such as smartphones, drones, and industrial equipment. Both types of sensors play a vital role in high-performance applications that demand accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
