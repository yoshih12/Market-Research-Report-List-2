<p><p>Referral management involves different market types, including self-referral, professional-referral, and third-party referral markets. In self-referral, patients seek care directly without a recommendation. Professional-referral involves healthcare providers directing patients to other specialists or services within a network. Third-party referral markets involve external entities like insurance companies or employers referring patients to specific providers. Each type plays a crucial role in guiding patients to the appropriate care and services, ultimately improving health outcomes and patient satisfaction.</p></p>
