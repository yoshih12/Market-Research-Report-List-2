<p><p>The non-invasive sphygmomanometer market consists of three main types: automatic sphygmomanometers, mercury sphygmomanometers, and aneroid sphygmomanometers. Automatic sphygmomanometers are electronic devices that inflate and deflate automatically to measure blood pressure. Mercury sphygmomanometers use a column of mercury to measure blood pressure. Aneroid sphygmomanometers have a dial gauge that provides a visual representation of blood pressure readings. Each type has its own unique features and advantages, catering to different preferences and needs of healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
