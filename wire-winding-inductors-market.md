<p><p>Wire-winding inductors are classified into two main types based on their core material: ceramic core wire-winding inductors and magnetic core wire-winding inductors. Ceramic core wire-winding inductors are made with a ceramic material, providing stability and high frequency characteristics. On the other hand, magnetic core wire-winding inductors use a magnetic material such as ferrite or iron, offering high inductance and efficiency. Both types serve different purposes in electronic devices and are used in various applications based on their specific properties and performance requirements.</p></p>
