<p><p>Lime desiccants are used to absorb moisture and maintain low humidity levels in various industries. There are two main types of lime desiccants: physical desiccants and chemical desiccants. Physical desiccants work by physically absorbing moisture through mechanisms like adsorption or absorption. Chemical desiccants, on the other hand, work by chemically binding with water molecules to remove moisture from the air. Both types play a crucial role in preserving the quality of products and materials by preventing moisture-related damage.</p></p>
