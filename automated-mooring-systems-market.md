<p><ul><li>Mooring Lines</li><li>No Mooring Lines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated mooring systems can be classified into two main types based on the presence of mooring lines: mooring lines and no mooring lines. Mooring lines refer to traditional systems that use ropes or cables to secure a vessel to the dock. On the other hand, no mooring lines systems utilize innovative technology such as vacuum suction or magnetic attraction to securely hold the vessel in place without the need for physical lines. Both types offer efficient and reliable mooring solutions for different marine applications.</p></p>
