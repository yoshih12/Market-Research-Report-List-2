<p><p>A Combined Cycle Steam Turbine is a power generation system that combines gas turbines and steam turbines to achieve higher efficiency and overall performance. The gas turbine generates electricity by burning fuel and producing hot gases, which are then used to produce steam in a heat recovery steam generator. The steam is then utilized to drive a steam turbine, generating additional electricity. This combined cycle approach significantly increases the efficiency of the power generation process, making it a popular choice for power plants worldwide.</p><p>The Combined Cycle Steam Turbine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period.
