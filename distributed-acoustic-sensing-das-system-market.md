<p><strong>The Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing</li><li>Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) System market includes two main types: Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing and Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing. Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing utilizes sensors to detect compression and rarefaction waves along the length of the fiber optic cable, providing data on sound waves traveling in the same direction as the fiber. Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing, on the other hand, detects vibrations perpendicular to the fiber's direction, offering insights into different types of acoustic disturbances. These two market segments cater to distinct applications and industries.</p></p>
