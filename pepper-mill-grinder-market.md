<p><p>The pepper mill grinder market can be divided into two main types: manual and electric. Manual pepper grinders require physical effort to grind the peppercorns, offering more control over the coarseness of the grind. Electric pepper grinders, on the other hand, run on batteries or electricity and require less effort to operate. They are often preferred for their convenience and speed. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with manual grinders being favored by those who enjoy the traditional grinding experience, while electric grinders are popular for their ease of use.</p></p>
