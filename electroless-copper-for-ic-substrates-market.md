<p><strong>The Electroless Copper for IC Substrates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Electroless Copper</li><li>Vertical Electroless Copper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal Electroless Copper for IC substrates involves the deposition of a thin layer of copper onto a flat surface, providing a smooth and uniform coating. This method is commonly used in PCB manufacturing to create conductive traces. On the other hand, Vertical Electroless Copper involves depositing copper onto a vertical surface, such as the sidewalls of vias. This process is essential for creating connections between different layers in a PCB, ensuring efficient signal transmission. Both types of Electroless Copper play crucial roles in the production of high-performance IC substrates.</p></p>
