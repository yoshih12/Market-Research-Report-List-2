<p><p>Rubber Filling Oil market is categorized into three types based on their chemical composition: Naphthenic Base, Paraffin Base, and Aromatic Base. Naphthenic Base oils provide good thermal stability and low temperature performance. Paraffin Base oils have high viscosity index and excellent oxidation resistance. Aromatic Base oils are known for their high solvency and good compatibility with additives. These different types cater to various requirements in the rubber industry, offering diverse benefits and performance characteristics.</p></p>
