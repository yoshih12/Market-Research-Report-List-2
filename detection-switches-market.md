<p><p>Detection switches market types like SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) and SPST-NC (Single Pole Single Throw Normally Closed) are commonly used in various industries. SPDT switches have one input terminal and two outputs, allowing for switching between two separate circuits. On the other hand, SPST-NC switches are normally closed, meaning the circuit is complete in its default state and opens when activated. Both types offer versatility in switching applications and are known for their reliability and durability in different environments.</p></p>
