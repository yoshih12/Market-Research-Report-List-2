<p><p>Electromagnetic wave absorbers can be divided into two main types in the market: resonant absorbers and broadband absorbers. Resonant absorbers are designed to absorb specific frequencies by utilizing resonant circuits or materials with specific resonant frequencies. On the other hand, broadband absorbers are capable of absorbing a wide range of frequencies without being limited to specific resonant frequencies. Both types of absorbers are widely used in various industries to reduce electromagnetic interference and improve signal quality in electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
