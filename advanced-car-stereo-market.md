<p><p>The Advanced Car Stereo market can be divided into two main types - OEM and Aftermarket. OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) refers to car stereos that come pre-installed by the car manufacturer. Aftermarket car stereos, on the other hand, are purchased separately and installed after the car is bought. OEM car stereos are often more integrated with the car's systems, while aftermarket stereos offer more customization options and upgraded features. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of consumers in the car audio market.</p></p>
