<p><strong>The Automotive Specialty Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>Powder Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive specialty coatings market includes solvent-borne, waterborne, and powder coating types. Solvent-borne coatings use volatile organic compounds as carriers, offering high durability and gloss. Waterborne coatings are environmentally friendly, using water as a carrier instead of solvents, reducing VOC emissions. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and cured under heat, providing a durable and high-quality finish. Each type offers unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different needs and preferences within the automotive industry.</p></p>
