<p><p>Genetically Modified Organisms (GMO) Testing market includes two main types of testing technologies: Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) Technology and Immunoassay Technology. PCR Technology is used to detect specific DNA sequences in GMOs, providing accurate and sensitive results. Immunoassay Technology, on the other hand, works by detecting antibodies that bind to specific GMO proteins. Both technologies play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and regulatory compliance of GMO products in industries such as agriculture and food production.</p></p>
