<p><p>Automated terminal tractors come in two main types: 4*2 and 6*4. The 4*2 configuration refers to a vehicle with four wheels, two of which are powered, providing sufficient traction for moving trailers and containers around a terminal. On the other hand, the 6*4 configuration involves six wheels, with four of them powered, offering enhanced stability and towing capacity for heavier loads. Both types are commonly used in shipping terminals, warehouses, and distribution centers for efficient and automated transportation of goods.</p></p>
