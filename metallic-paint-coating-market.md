<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallic paint and coating market can be categorized into two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based metallic paints are formulated using organic solvents that evaporate during the curing process, resulting in a durable and glossy finish. On the other hand, water-based metallic paints use water as a base instead of solvents, making them eco-friendly and less toxic. Both types offer unique properties and benefits, catering to different consumer preferences and application requirements in the market.</p></p>
